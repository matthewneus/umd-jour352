Grilled Cheese

This classic grilled cheese sandwich recipe calls for just white bread, sliced cheese, and butter. It's easy to switch up the bread and cheese to suit your tast preferences and, if you like, you can substitute mayonnaise for butter.

Best Cheese for Grilled Cheese

This recipe calls for sliced Cheddar, which is a wonderful crowd-pleasing cheese. You can use sharp Cheddar, mild Cheddar, or a blend of both.

Ingredients

  • White Bread: Two slices
  • Butter: Three tablespoons
  • Cheddar Cheese: Two Slices

How to Make the Best Grilled Cheese

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillit
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up)
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted

Tips and Tricks

Variations

Try pairing different types of breads and cheeses to find the perfect sandwich.

Add-Ons

Making homemade tomato soup can elevate this recipe to the next level.
