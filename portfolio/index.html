Hello, I'm Matthew Neus

I'm a young, ambitious student studying journalism at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland, College Park.

I currently write for The Diamondback, the independent student newspaper at my university. I contribute to the sports desk, taking on the Maryland men's soccer beat currently and recently working on the Maryland wrestling beat.

When I'm not typing a story on my laptop, you can find me in front of a camera. I am an Associate Producer for Terrapin Sports Central, helping cover Maryland athletics and produce weekly studio shows to keep our viewers informed on everything Maryland sports.

When I'm not reporting, I enjoy reading the news and spending time with family and friends.
