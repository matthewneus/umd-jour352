Published Clips

Here are some of my samples that I enjoyed reporting the most, or that I am most proud of.

Vaughan Soccer Club has supplied Maryland men's soccer with a wealth of Canadian talent

In this story, I explored the relationship between Maryland men's soccer and Canadian youth soccer club Vaughan S.C. Current players Kimani Stewart-Baynes and Stefan Copetti played at the club, and former Terps Dayne St. Clair and Malcolm Johnston did as well.

Maryland men's soccer graduated its leaders. Stefan Copetti has helped fill the void.

Following a long list of program leaders, senior forward Stefan Copetti is relishing the opportunity to lead a young Maryland men's soccer team entering the 2023 fall season.

Jaxon, Jaron Smith have a bond built on a love for wrestling and raw egg yolks

Jaxon Smith wrestled at Maryland for eight seasons. Jaxon Smith entered the 2022-23 season as a freshman. The heavyweight duo live together on the mat, with that relationship strenghted by participation in one weird weight-gaining technique.

Freshman Leon Koehl, with veteran aide, is starring for Maryland men's soccer

I dove into the latest addition of Leon Koehl, a freshman midfielder who spent his youth career in Germany for Bayer Leverkusen. He arrived two weeks before the start of the Terps' season, and recently developed a partnership with fellow German midfielder Alex Nitzl.

Dominic Solis chose wrestling over football. Now, he's Maryland's leader.

Dominic Solis played football at McDonogh in the fall, and wrestled in the winter. When he arrived at Maryland, he made the choice to put on a singlet instead of the football pads. He's now leading Maryland wrestling in his final years with the program.
